# ChildPulse (Rebuilt)
### One Photo. One Voice Note. One Life.

---

## 🧭 Overview

**ChildPulse** is an AI-powered child health triage and disease surveillance platform designed for low-resource environments. It combines computer vision, multilingual voice analysis, and clinical protocols to help frontline health workers quickly assess, diagnose, and respond to pediatric illness.

Using just **one photo** and **one voice note**, ChildPulse generates clinically meaningful insights, supports treatment decisions, and contributes to real-time outbreak detection.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Community-level healthcare systems face major challenges:

- Rigid checklist-based assessments  
- Loss of symptom timelines and severity nuances  
- Language barriers between caregivers and health workers  
- Limited training among frontline workers  
- Lack of real-time disease surveillance  

These gaps lead to delayed diagnosis, missed danger signs, and poor public health visibility.

---

## 💡 Core Insight

> The mother is the most accurate observer of her child’s condition.

Instead of forcing structured inputs, ChildPulse allows caregivers to **speak freely**, capturing richer and more natural health information.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

### 📸 1. Photo Capture

A single image of the child (face and upper body) is analyzed.

**AI extracts:**
- Signs of malnutrition (visible wasting)  
- Pallor (possible anemia or malaria)  
- Sunken eyes (dehydration)  
- Skin discoloration (jaundice, cyanosis)  
- Hair abnormalities (kwashiorkor indicators)  
- Edema (swelling)  
- Skin rashes (e.g., measles patterns)  
- Estimated age  
- General alertness  

---

### 🎤 2. Voice Note

The caregiver records a 30–60 second voice note in any language.

**AI extracts:**
- Symptoms (fever, cough, vomiting, etc.)  
- Timeline and duration  
- Severity indicators (e.g., inability to drink)  
- Associated symptoms  
- Behavioral changes  
- Emotional urgency signals  

---

## 🧠 Clinical Intelligence Engine

ChildPulse integrates:

- WHO iCCM guidelines  
- Regional disease prevalence models  
- Pediatric drug dosing standards  
- Nutritional assessment criteria  
- Local referral pathways  

---

## 📊 Outputs

- **Diagnosis Card** (condition + severity level)  
- **Treatment Guidance** (immediate and pre-referral actions)  
- **Nutritional Flags** (malnutrition indicators)  
- **Referral Recommendation** (facility + urgency)  

---

## 📡 Smart Referral System

- Identifies nearest appropriate health facility  
- Sends SMS alerts with patient summary  
- Reduces delays in emergency response

## 🌍 Outbreak Intelligence

Each case contributes to a real-time dataset including:

- Location (GPS)  
- Time  
- Disease classification  
- Severity  

### Detection includes:
- Case clustering  
- Sudden spikes  
- Disease pattern shifts  
- Malnutrition hotspots
